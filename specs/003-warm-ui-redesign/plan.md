Pure visual redesign across 4 existing screens and 1 component. Introduces a shared design-token file (`src/constants/theme.js`) to centralise the warm palette (#FF6B35 accent, #FFFAF7 background) and applies it consistently to `CameraScreen`, `RecipeStackScreen`, `RecipeDetailScreen`, `RecipeCard`, and `RecipeDetailScreen`.
